Rust includes the primitive types,1 statements, and expressions2 you're already familiar with. However, it also incorporates some powerful concepts from functional languages that we will explore throughout this course. To begin with our rustle program, we'll keep things simple and gradually refactor the code to introduce more of what Rust has to offer. Let's start with variables.
